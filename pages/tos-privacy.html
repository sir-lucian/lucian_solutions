1) Scope & Acceptance

This policy explains how LuciaBot collects, uses, discloses, and retains data necessary for operation on Discord. By inviting or using the bot, you accept this policy and applicable Discord policies.

2) Data We Collect

We process only minimal necessary data, such as:

  • User/Server/Channel IDs, role names, admin-configured settings
  • Command logs (timestamps/results) for responses and troubleshooting
  • Stream statuses required for notifications (e.g., online/offline/announcement)
  • Command-related message snippets strictly as needed for features to function

We do not intend to collect sensitive personal data beyond what is necessary and avoid storing unrelated private content.

3) Purposes of Use

We use data to:

  • Operate core features: stream notifications, random food command, role management
  • Maintain reliability and fix issues
  • Improve service quality (e.g., aggregate/de-identified analytics)

If additional purposes arise, we will communicate beforehand where feasible.

4) Disclosures to Third Parties

We do not disclose data to third parties except where necessary, such as:

  • Connecting to supported platforms/APIs to deliver stream alerts as configured
  • Compliance with applicable laws/regulations/court orders
  • Fraud prevention and security incident detection/response

Where possible, data will be aggregated or de-identified prior to disclosure.

5) Retention & Storage Location

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide services and troubleshoot, then delete or anonymize it per reasonable schedules. Storage/hosting details: Docker, Koyeb.

6) Security Measures

We implement reasonable safeguards, such as access controls, encryption where appropriate, event logging, and least-privilege permissioning. No system is 100% secure; admins should configure bot and Discord role permissions carefully.

7) Data Subject Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, objection/restriction of processing, and data portability by contacting us. We will respond within reasonable timeframes and processes.

8) Children & Minors

LuciaBot is intended for users per Discord's age requirements. We do not knowingly collect data from minors below the threshold. If inadvertent collection occurs, we will delete upon notification where appropriate.

9) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time and will communicate changes via our website. Continued use after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
